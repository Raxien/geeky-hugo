A round number. Just a number, one might say.
But the **sheet metal**, the **tires**, and even a few hidden parts under the chassis know better: we sweated for this milestone.

We've just passed **100,000 km travelled aboard our van**.
A small world on wheels that, since May 2022, has become our home, our ship, our shelter.

These days we've had to do some maintenance work — nothing serious, [but the van asked for a bit of attention](/van) (and we gave it). We'll talk about it soon in a dedicated video.

As for the rest...
Every kilometer has been a weave of **emotions, encounters, discoveries, lessons**.
Some lived in silence, while others left a deeper mark that we'll carry with us forever.

We don't know how many kilometers still lie ahead,
but if they're even a little like the ones behind us,
then we can't wait to keep going down the road.

📍 Thank you to everyone who follows us, who writes to us, who gives us precious time or a smile along the way.
**The beauty of this journey is also (above all) shared.**
