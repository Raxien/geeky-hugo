## How It Works

1. Request Your Tickets: Log in to the Japan Bullet Train website and select the trains you want to book.
2. Receive a QR Code: Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive a QR code by email.
3. Collect Your Tickets: Show the QR code at a designated JR station to receive your paper tickets.

If you make a mistake when booking (for example selecting the wrong date), you can make changes by going to a JR station. The cost for a change is 400 yen.
