After living in Piedmont and travelling by plane, car and train, in May 2022 we decided to turn our everyday life upside down and live for an indefinite period on board a van.

The {{<extLink "Roadcar 540" "https://www.roadcar-mobile.de/it/modelli/roadcar/roadcar-r540">}}, Possl's entry-level model, seen and bought from {{<extLink "Beltramo Campers" "https://www.beltramocampers.com/roadcar">}} in Cuneo, won us over from day one. Few frills, essential but just right.
We were, and still are, missing those two or three things to make it perform the way we need. Only by actually living on board and weighing every truly necessary need did we decide whether or not to invest in certain accessories, without chasing trends or flashy upgrades.
We also didn't want to replace the original accessories right away, preferring to use them until the very end to avoid pointlessly wasteful purchases.

On this page you'll find all the [information](#il-van-in-breve) about our van, [the story](#storia-e-evoluzione-del-van) of its evolution, and all the [costs incurred](#i-costi-nel-dettaglio) (purchase and maintenance).
